Hello! I am a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Alan Yuille. My research interests lie in the fields of 3D computer vision, robustness, and robotics.

Before that, I received my Bachelors from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I have also spent great time at Bytedance (with Dr. Song Bai).
